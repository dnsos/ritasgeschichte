Rita

The story of an egg-worker

Project

Told here is the story of Rita, a laying hen who experiences the different forms and stages of the egg industry. But Rita is no regular „egg-worker“. Little by little, she discovers her own basic instincts and does not hesitate to fight for them.

This project aims to provide an overview of regulations and conditions in the different stages of the laying hen industry (hatchery, barn, free range, organic) in Germany. These are encountered with positions that are predominantly derived from the lineage of the laying hens ancestors, the junglefowls.

Initially the project was created in german language, therefore all cited sources are only available in German.

Background

The project is a student work, created in 2017 as part of the course „Hühner à la carte“, a cooperation between the University of Applied Sciences Potsdam, Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin and the IRI THESys institute.

Thanks to the interdisciplinary cooperation it was possible to present the project in several exhibitions .
